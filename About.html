Introduction

Hi there! I'm Shubhnish Verma, a passionate and detail-oriented recent graduate delving into the exciting world of web development. Graduating with a B.Tech in Computer Science in 2023, I bring a fresh perspective and a hunger for creative problem-solving to the table.

My journey into the tech realm has been characterized by a love for crafting seamless and visually appealing web experiences. Specializing in web design, UI/UX, and e-commerce solutions, I've honed my skills in Python, Django, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. Whether it's translating ideas into elegant code or navigating the intricacies of front-end and back-end development, I find joy in every step of the process.

Beyond academics, I've immersed myself in practical projects to apply my knowledge. From designing intuitive user interfaces to developing robust e-commerce platforms, each project has been a stepping stone towards refining my craft.

Looking forward, I'm eager to contribute my skills and creativity to collaborative projects that push the boundaries of what's possible in web development. I thrive in dynamic environments that foster continuous learning and growth, and I'm excited about the endless possibilities that technology presents.
